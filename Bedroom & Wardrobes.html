Our Services

Bedroom Interiors & Wardrobes

About the service

At Grain & Glory, we create modern bedroom interiors in Chennai that are designed for comfort, relaxation, and style. Our approach focuses on personalized bedroom spaces that reflect your lifestyle while offering smart storage and a clutter-free environment. From cozy master bedrooms to compact guest rooms and functional kids’ bedrooms, we bring you innovative interior design solutions tailored for every type of home.

We specialize in modular wardrobes in Chennai, offering a wide range of wardrobe designs including sliding door wardrobes, hinged wardrobes, mirrored wardrobes, L-shaped corner wardrobes, and walk-in wardrobes. Using high-quality materials like marine plywood, laminates, and acrylic finishes, we build wardrobes that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also durable and space-efficient. Every inch is planned to optimize usability—whether it’s adding lofts, hidden storage, or dedicated sections for accessories, shoes, and garments.

Grain & Glory provides end-to-end bedroom design and wardrobe solutions, including lighting, false ceiling, wall panelling, painting, and furnishing. Whether you prefer a minimal aesthetic or a luxurious vibe, we’ll transform your bedroom into a serene, functional space.

  1. Customized modular wardrobe design and installation.
  2. Plywood wardrobes with laminate, acrylic, or PU finish.
  3. Sliding, hinged, and walk-in wardrobe options.
  4. Bed back panels, side tables, study units, and TV panels.
  5. Smart storage solutions and space optimization.
  6. Ambient lighting and ceiling design for a relaxed vibe.
  7. Bedroom renovation and turnkey interior execution.
What's part of the service?

Our bedroom interior design service in Chennai includes site analysis, layout planning, 3D visualization, and expert material selection. We handle everything from wardrobe fabrication to false ceiling, electrical fitting, and wall textures or wallpapers. Whether you're upgrading an existing bedroom or designing a new one from scratch, our team ensures perfect synergy between form and function.

Searching for a reliable wardrobe manufacturer near you or a bedroom renovation expert in Chennai? Grain & Glory delivers complete bedroom transformation with beautiful finishes, premium craftsmanship, and a commitment to timelines and transparency.
