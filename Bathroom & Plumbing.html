Our Services

Bathroom Renovation & Plumbing

About the service

At Grain & Glory, we specialize in bathroom renovation in Chennai that combines smart space planning, modern fixtures, and flawless finishes. Whether you need a small bathroom makeover or a complete transformation, we design bathrooms that are stylish, practical, and built to last. From anti-skid tiles and concealed flush systems to custom vanity units and shower enclosures, we take care of every detail.

Our plumbing services in Chennai ensure that the foundation of your bathroom is strong and leak-free. We handle complete plumbing line overhauls, CP and sanitary fitting replacements, concealed pipeline installations, and waterproofing solutions. Our experienced team addresses common issues like water seepage, poor drainage, and low pressure with precision and care.

With our turnkey bathroom renovation services, you don’t have to coordinate with multiple vendors. We take care of everything—from demolition and civil work to tiling, plumbing, electrical, and final fittings. We also recommend and install premium bathroom brands to ensure durability and style.

  1. End-to-end bathroom renovation and remodeling.
  2. Concealed plumbing and sanitary ware installation.
  3. Anti-skid floor tiles and designer wall tiles.
  4. Wall-mounted WC, vanity cabinets, and glass partitions.
  5. Complete waterproofing and drainage corrections.
  6. Upgrade to smart fixtures and space-saving solutions.
  7. Bathroom repairs, fitting replacement & leakproof solutions.
What's part of the service?

Our bathroom renovation solutions include design planning, material selection, plumbing layout, tile setting, and fixture fitting. We ensure your bathroom is not just functional but also easy to maintain and visually appealing. Whether you need bathroom upgrades for homes, apartments, or rental properties, Grain & Glory delivers hassle-free execution with guaranteed quality.

Searching for best bathroom interior designers near me or reliable plumbing services in Chennai? We’re here to bring your dream bathroom to life—stylish, clean, and absolutely leak-free!
