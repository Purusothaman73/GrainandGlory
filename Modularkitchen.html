Our Services

Modular kitchen

About the service

Grain & Glory specializes in crafting custom modular kitchens in Chennai that blend modern design, ergonomic efficiency, and long-lasting durability. Our modular kitchen interior design services are ideal for both new constructions and kitchen renovations. Whether you prefer a sleek contemporary look or a classic traditional style, our team ensures your kitchen is tailored to match your cooking habits, lifestyle, and space requirements.

We understand that the kitchen is the heart of your home. That’s why we focus on every detail—from optimized storage solutions to easy-to-clean finishes, waterproof cabinets, and pest-resistant materials. Our expert designers work with you to create a layout that maximizes space and enhances workflow. Each kitchen is equipped with modular cabinets, soft-close drawers, built-in organizers, and top-notch accessories to simplify daily tasks and add a touch of elegance.

Whether you're in an apartment, independent house, or villa, we offer end-to-end modular kitchen installation in Chennai, including electrical work, plumbing, false ceiling, and lighting. With a focus on quality and functionality, we ensure your kitchen is not just beautiful—but also built to last.

  1. Custom kitchen cabinets and units.
  2. Termite-proof and moisture-resistant plywood.
  3. Granite, quartz, and acrylic countertop options.
  4. Branded hardware and soft-close fittings.
  5. Seamless integration of chimney, hob, and appliances.
  6. Waterproof finishes and anti-skid flooring (optional).
  7. Electrical and plumbing setup with safety compliance.
What's part of the service?

Our modular kitchen service in Chennai includes complete turnkey solutions—from concept to completion. We handle layout planning, 3D visualizations, cabinet manufacturing, countertop installation, tiling, electrical, and plumbing. We also provide expert assistance in choosing the best kitchen accessories, color schemes, handles, lighting, and finishes that align with your personal taste and budget. Our team manages everything in-house, ensuring timely delivery, flawless execution, and a zero-hassle experience.

Looking for a trusted modular kitchen contractor near you? Grain & Glory is your one-stop destination for modular kitchen design and renovation services in Chennai. Get in touch for a site visit and personalized consultation.
